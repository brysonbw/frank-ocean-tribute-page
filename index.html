Frank Ocean

One of the Greatest Musical/Recording Artist of the 21st century

Timeline of Frank Ocean's life:


  • 1987 - (Frank Ocean) Born Christopher Edwin Breaux on October 28, 1987, in Long Beach, California.
  • 2005 - Graduates high school and enrolls at the Univeristy of New Orleans. However, primarly focuses on music.
  • 2008 - Moves to Los Angeles to contiune recording projects. Eventually, lands a songwriting deal and begins to collaborate with producers. In the same year, works with Los Angeles-based hip-hop collective Odd Future, and signs a contract with Def Jam as a solo artist.
  • 2011 - Releases a mixtape titled nostalgia, ULTRA.
  • 2012 - Riding the success of nostalgia, ULTRA, releases second album channel ORANGE.
  • 2013 - channel, ORANGE tops the music charts and wins the 55th Annual Grammy Award for best urban contemporary album.
  • 2016 - Releases the visual album Endless and third album (second studio album), Blonde.
